Ownership

Ownership begins with consideration, not transaction.

There is no cart, no checkout, no price list. Acquiring a Varenyam creation follows one of four paths — each beginning with a conversation.

Path One

Private Consultation

A confidential meeting, in person or by appointment, to discuss a creation currently marked as privately available. We learn about the collector as much as the collector learns about the piece.

Path Two

Bespoke Commission

For collectors seeking a creation built around a specific motif, family heritage, or occasion. Commissions are limited to one or two a year, given the months of work required.

Path Three

Request for Consideration

An open inquiry to the house, reviewed personally, that places a prospective collector into conversation for future or current creations.

Path Four

Founding Circle Access

An invitation-only relationship offering early visibility into the house's annual creations, ahead of any private consultation.

An Invitation, Not A Membership Fee

The Founding Circle

The Founding Circle is a small, invitation-only group of collectors and patrons who support Varenyam's work in preserving Banarasi craftsmanship. Entry is not purchased — it is extended, by the house, to those whose relationship with the craft warrants it.
